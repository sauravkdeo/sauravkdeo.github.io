Hi, I'm Saurav !

I am currently pursuing Masters in Robotics specialization at University of Maryland, college Park. My research interests are in the field of path planning, perception and implementing ML and RL algorithms for autonomous maneuvers.

Prior to university of Maryland, I worked at Honda R&D India, where I evaluated and validated Honda two-wheeler engines.

Before Honda R&D India, I was Manager in R&D Prototype and testing team at Daimler India. During my tenure at Daimler, I was responsible for the development of design test plan, validation and, functional evaluation of power-train system for the prototype vehicles including Bharatbenz, Fuso and Mercedes Benz Trucks.

While working in automobile R&D industry, I developed interest in the autonomous vehicles and decided to pursue my passion in that field.

Currently, I keep myself busy in developing skill sets required for autonomous systems.

Nearly all of my code is open source, and I enjoy contributing to other open source projects.

I primarily code in c++ and Python, but dabble in other languages.

Other interesting tidbits:

  • I completed my Bachelors from National Institute of Technology, Silchar in Mechanical Engineering in year 2013.
  • I'm a decent table tennis player, an enthusiastic traveler, and a decent home cook.
